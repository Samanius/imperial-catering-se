## Menu Types

The restaurants feature three menu presentation styles:

- **Visual Menu** (à la carte): Individual dishes with images, prices, and descriptions
- **Tasting Menu** (PDF style): Multi-course fixed menu with detailed progression
- **Both**: Restaurants offering both visual and tasting menu options

## Features Included

All restaurants come pre-configured with:
- Cover images and gallery photos
- Menu items with weights (in grams)
- Category organization
- Minimum order amounts
- Order deadline hours (24-72 hours before charter)
- Chef service pricing
- Waiter service pricing per person

## How to Load

From the Admin Panel:
1. Click "Load Sample Restaurants"
2. Confirm the action
3. Sample restaurants will be added to your catalog
4. Existing restaurants won't be duplicated

All changes are automatically backed up in the system.
